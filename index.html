I was born in Vilnius Lithuania in 1994. I moved to London to study Photography in Middlesex University in 2012. focusing on documentary, journalistic and artistic genres. I did my final project under the direction of Werner Mahler, showing a visual history among women. I took part in different workshops with Ute Mahler (fashion workshop in Halle, Germany) and Alex Webb and Rebecca Norris (“The art of editing” at Fotografiska Museum, Stockholm). Since then I work as an independent photographer paying attention to the human being and the sporadic.
